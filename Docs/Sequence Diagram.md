## Sequence Diagram

<img width="1825" height="2533" alt="Picture3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a5f5249e-7be2-4d3e-8791-292cbd7a644b" />
This sequence illustrates how a meeting request is created and processed. The system validates that the receiver teaches the
selected module and checks their availability before saving the request. A notification is generated for the receiver, 
who can accept or decline the request. The sender is then updated with the final status. This ensures logical and consistent interactions.
